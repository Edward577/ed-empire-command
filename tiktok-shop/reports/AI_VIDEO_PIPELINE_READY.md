## Recommended Creator-Native Shot Patterns

### Dresses

- 0-2s: mirror try-on hook with full outfit visible immediately
- 2-5s: waist-to-hem movement or walk reveal
- 5-8s: close detail on neckline, slit, satin sheen, or fit
- 8-12s: daytime-to-night styling transition
- 12-15s: hold on best silhouette with soft CTA

### Matching Sets

- 0-2s: fast before/after change
- 2-5s: texture closeup and seated comfort moment
- 5-9s: coffee-run or hallway walk clip
- 9-12s: side profile fit check
- 12-15s: bag tap / add-to-cart CTA

### Outerwear

- 0-2s: throw-on transition
- 2-6s: sleeve, lining, trim, or collar macro
- 6-10s: layered outfit reveal
- 10-15s: casual street or doorway walkout
