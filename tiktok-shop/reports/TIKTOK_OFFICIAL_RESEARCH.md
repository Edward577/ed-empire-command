## What TikTok Itself Recommends

### 1. Think TikTok-first

TikTok’s official guidance says the content should feel native to the platform, not like a polished traditional ad.
That supports the current creator-style direction already chosen in this repo.

### 2. Use a simple structure

TikTok’s Creative Codes guidance says the strongest videos follow:

- hook
- body
- close

That means every product clip should:

- show the product in the first second
- prove the fit, texture, or use case in the middle
- finish with a direct CTA

### 3. Vertical and high resolution

TikTok’s official production guidance says to:

- shoot or render vertical 9:16
- use high-resolution footage
- leave space for TikTok UI

### 4. Sound matters

TikTok explicitly recommends building with music, voiceover, and sound effects.
For this lane, that means:

- don’t rely on silent slideshow edits
- use voiceover, on-screen captions, or both
- make the CTA audible or text-visible

### 5. Keep multiple creatives active

The Shop Ads Playbook says to keep multiple active creatives and not rely on a single video.
For this repo, that supports the current 5-video daily queue model.

### 6. Add product-focused CTA

TikTok’s Shop Ads Playbook recommends clear shopping CTA language and linking products consistently.
For this lane, every clip should end with a simple shopping instruction:

- tap the bag
- shop this look
- comment for the link

### 7. Product catalog quality still matters

TikTok catalog guidance says titles and descriptions should be precise, distinct, and high quality.
That supports keeping `optimized_listings.md` as the paste-ready truth before review submission.

## Repo-Level Implications

These findings support the current direction already in progress:

- realistic creator-style content over sterile catalog visuals
- 9:16 vertical clips
- hook/body/close format
- product visible immediately
- clear CTA ending
- multiple active creatives, not one hero clip
- better to keep posting current live products than wait on blocked source imports

## Applied Rules For This Repo

1. Keep the current live dress lane as the main posting lane
2. Run at least 5 active creatives, not 1 or 2
3. Use hook/body/close on every script
4. Keep sound and captions on every video
5. Use realistic creator energy, not over-produced catalog energy
6. Keep product links and product CTA behavior aligned with TikTok Shop usage
